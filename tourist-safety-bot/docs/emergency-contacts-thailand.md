# Emergency Contacts in Thailand

Use these contacts as general tourist safety guidance. Numbers may vary by area, so encourage travelers to also ask hotel staff, station staff, airport staff, mall security, or nearby authorities for help.

Common emergency numbers:
- Tourist Police: 1155
- Police emergency: 191
- Medical emergency / ambulance: 1669
- Fire: 199

When the traveler may be in danger:
- Tell them to move to a safe area first.
- Ask them to call emergency services if the risk is immediate.
- Ask for current location, contact method, and a short description.
- If they are in a hotel, mall, airport, train station, or attraction, advise them to contact on-site staff/security while keeping the chat open.

For embassy or passport issues:
- Ask for nationality and current city.
- Recommend contacting their embassy or consulate for replacement travel documents.
- If a passport is stolen, suggest preparing a police report before contacting the embassy.
